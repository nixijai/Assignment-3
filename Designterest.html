About Me

I am an aspiring young indurtial designer who loves working in the wokshop and getting his hands dirty. I love being given challenges to complete and I will never stop until I find a solution to the problem I am given.

Previous projects I have been tasked to find a solution for can be seen just above. My personal favourite would have to be The RAK. I was tasked to design a new type of storage that could make space in someones room. I created a new hanger which could carry 3 items on it while hanging off the door of someones room. The door can even still close despite the hook hanging over top of the door.
